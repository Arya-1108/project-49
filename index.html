Interresting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are parts of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleon have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Interresting Facts about Camel

  • They are two different species. Asian camels, known as Bactrian camels, have two humps, while Arabian camels, known as Dromedary camels, have one.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Wide feet and thick leathery pads help them to walk on the hot sand and keep them from sinking.

Interresting Facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas are harmless.they do not bite or sting
  • Most 17 Year Cicadas have red eyes, but they can also have white, gray, blue , or multi-colored eyes
  • Cicadas need to be warm to sing and fly around, but they’re cold-blooded. Their dark skin absorbs the heat of the sun, which helps to warm them up.

Interresting Facts about panda

  • An adult panda weight more than 45 kilos (100 pounds), and can be about 1.5 meters (5 feet) long!
  • A 45-kilo adult (and pandas can reach 150 kg in captivity) spends as long as 14 hours eating. And it can eat 12 to 38 kilos of bamboo a day.
  • “Giant panda” is actually just a nickname for a panda; they are no specifically larger than others.

Interresting Facts about pigeon

  • The pigeons have been around humans for thousands of year.
  • Pigeons can reach top speeds of up to 77.6mph for short periods and have been recorded flying as fast as 92.5 mph!
  • Scientists have discovered that the common pigeon actually has an astonishingly good long-term memory.
